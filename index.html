ABOUT US

INTRODUCTION

“MSP CONSULTING ENGINEER”
is a fully integrated
engineering company with its own
professional staff in all the main
technical & managerial disciplines of
modern engineering.

OUR GOALS
AND TECHNOLOGY

Our goal is simple – to achieve the client’s objectives
every time. Our success can best to measured by
how well the design solution meets the specific user
program requirements, budgetary considerations,
schedule limitations and quality of life concerns. It is
our experience at leading such efforts that has
taught us to evaluate design and has made us
committed to providing both quality and value.

We integrate automation, computer application and
systemization into our own internal office operations
whenever possible. As a group we are highly
automated with each office committed to using the
best available technology particularly computerized
design and drafting. Strategically, we want our
people to have the most effective and efficient
means to work directly on all phases of a project.
The clear purpose is to extend the efforts of the best
people on behalf our clients.
